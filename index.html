Kristen Watson


Kristen Watson is a multimedia journalist, photographer and designer in the DFW area.


She served as editor and photographer for Alexis Bortell’s book, Let’s Talk About Medical Cannabis and created The Illegal Hope Project, a photojournalism project that aims to document medical marijuana refugee’s stories as well as stories of patients stuck in non-medical states.


Kristen is a graduate of the University of North Texas’ Mayborn School of Journalism and is currently completing a full-stack development bootcamp at Southern Methodist University. She is also currently serving as a User Experience Design Fellow at brand and design agency, Six Things.


Her work has been published by the Dallas Morning News, USA Today, Mother Jones, Mashable, WFAA, CBS DFW, Denton Record-Chronicle, The Dentonite, NeighborsGO, Texas Woman’s University and the North Texas Daily.
